info
    args: info {thing}
    prints information about a specified thing
spy
    args: spy {property}
    learn information about a specific property of the soviet union
    less likely to fail with a stronger military
step
    args: step [number of months]
    steps forward in time a certain number of months
    defaults to 1
    all that plays out is resource production, trade, and betterment/worsening of things depending on funds
quit
    args: quit
    quits the game
